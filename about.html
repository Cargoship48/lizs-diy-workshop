About Me

Hi, I'm Elizabeth, the maker of Squishes.

I love making things with my hands, and one day I figured out how to make my very own squishy toys. I call them Squishes!

Each one is made from plastic wrap and shaving cream, and then I add my favorite colors so no two are exactly the same. They feel really soft and squishy when you give them a poke.

Oh, and I really, really love ducks ♥. You'll probably spot a few of them around here.

Thanks for visiting my workshop. I hope you find a Squish that makes you smile!

Made with love by Elizabeth ♥ (and a few ducks)
